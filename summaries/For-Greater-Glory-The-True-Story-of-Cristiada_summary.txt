For Greater Glory: The True Story of the Cristeros is a 30 minute documentary that unveils the largely unknown story of the Cristiada, the uprising of Mexican Catholics against the harsh persecution of President Plutarco Calles and his regime in the 1920's. This documentary delves into the complex history and rich characters of this period through interviews with leading historian and Church leaders while also drawing from exclusive behind the scenes footage and interviews with the ensemble cast (Andy Garcia, Eva Longoria, Peter O'Toole) of the 2012 feature film "For Greater Glory."::Anonymous
