This documentary treats film fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Life as a House, about a man dying of cancer who resolves to make the most of what little time he has left by reaching out to his estranged son. Includes footage of the filming process, as well as interviews with stars Kevin Kline and Hayden Christensen, as well as other members of the cast and crew, who give their insights into what it was like working together on this project and the efforts it took to bring the film to completion.::Jean-Marc Rocher <rocher@fiberbit.net>
