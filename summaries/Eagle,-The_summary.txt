The Red Eagle takes place during the 1760's-1810's, where Portugal, France and England are all trying to take over Spain. A man named, "The Red Eagle", is the hero among people, a man who fights for justice, meets a beautiful blonde women, looking for the aid of The Red Eagle. Angered and grieved by a random accident, The Red Eagle decides to pull one last heroic deed. During all this, there is a summit going on between Spain, Portugal, France and England, during which something disastrous happens and it affects the whole city.
