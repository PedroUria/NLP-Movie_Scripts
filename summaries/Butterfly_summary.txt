Butterfly Crush is a modern age love story, set against the backdrop of the music industry, where the villains are a cult, into astrology and virtual dreaming, who peddle their wares on-line. The song and dance duo, Butterfly Crush, are up for the Australasian Song Awards, but their chance at success is jeopardized when Eva gets involved with the Kings Cross cult, the Dreamguides. Moana must rescue her and risk seduction by a young cult member, Matt.::Anonymous
