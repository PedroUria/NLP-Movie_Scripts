After a long and arduous divorce, Debra vacations at a quiet off-season beach to decompress and forget about men. Immediately, a pushy lifeguard hits on her, and she blows him off. Then his TWIN cowboy brother, a gentleman but a taker, then finally the TRIPLET artist brother whom she likes, until she finds out she's been had, and engages in a fun and intriguing game of tit for tat.::Valentine (XV)
