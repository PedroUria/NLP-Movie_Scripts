This documentary treats film fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Lone Wolf McQuade, a film about a Texas Ranger who vows to avenge his partner's death by going to war with a drug kingpin. Includes footage of the filming process, as well as interviews with star Chuck Norris and other members of the cast and crew, who give their insights into what it was like working together on this action project and the efforts it took to bring the film to completion.::Jean-Marc Rocher <rocher@fiberbit.net>
