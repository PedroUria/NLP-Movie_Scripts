Bogie pees on Blyss while she is walking him, while Blyss (played by Miss California USA 2014 Contestant Suzanne Starr Smith) continues to struggle though her weekend money making scheme. Blyss decides to rely on her friends for help, but quickly discovers there are not many options as nearly everyone is away for the weekend in Palm Springs at the hottest music festival of spring break in California; So Cal Spring Break Fest. Fiona (played by Emmy Award Winning Actress Rolanda Mendelle) and the neighbor go to Nikki's and "rescue" Bogie from Blyss. Blyss becomes desperate as her entrepreneurial plans for the weekend quickly unravel and she has to decided what to do.::Suzanne Starr Smith
