The Story of Sapphires is a short documentary about Sapphires Model Management, a boutique modelling agency based in Central London. The company was founded by two entrepreneurs from a small town in the English Midlands and this tell the journey from humble beginnings to owning a thriving business in the centre of one of the world's great cities. The documentary seeks to lift the lid on the realities of running a small business in the fashion world and dispels some of myths - while at the same time revealing the truths - about the fashion industry. Featuring interviews with models, businesspeople and some important people in fashion this is essential viewing for anyone who wants to know what it takes to make it in fashion.::Anonymous
