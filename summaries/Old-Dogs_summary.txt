After 50 years of marriage, Millie decides that she is sick of her husband Dewayne who is more interested in fishing and watching the weather channel than paying attention to her. After Millie leaves him, Dewayne experiences life without Millie and comes to realize that she meant more to him than just someone who made her meals. Off to the church Bingo game he goes to win her back, but will it be too late?::Anonymous
